UK Tier 2 Visa: A Comprehensive Guide



If you're planning to work in the United Kingdom, understanding the UK Tier 2 visa is essential. This visa category is designed for skilled workers from outside the European Economic Area (EEA) and Switzerland, allowing them to work in the UK when local candidates are not available. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about the UK Tier 2 visa, including eligibility criteria, the application process, and the benefits it offers.



What is the UK Tier 2 Visa?

The UK Tier 2 visa is a work visa for skilled workers from outside the EEA and Switzerland. It enables UK employers to fill job vacancies with skilled workers from abroad, ensuring that essential roles are filled by qualified individuals. This visa is crucial for bringing in expertise that is in demand in the UK.



Understanding the Points-Based System

The Tier 2 visa operates under a points-based system, which evaluates applicants based on their qualifications, skills, and sponsorship by a UK employer. The system ensures that applicants meet the necessary criteria to contribute effectively to the UK workforce. Key factors considered include having a job offer, English language proficiency, and sufficient maintenance funds.



Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a Tier 2 visa, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Job Offer from a UK Employer: You need a valid job offer from a UK employer who is licensed to sponsor you. The job must meet specific salary and skill requirements.
  • Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS): Your UK employer must issue a CoS, confirming they are sponsoring you for the job. This certificate includes important details about your role and sponsorship.
  • Appropriate Salary Level: Your job offer must meet the minimum salary threshold set by the UK government for your occupation. Salary requirements vary depending on the job role and the Standard Occupational Classification (SOC) code.
  • English Language Proficiency: You must demonstrate your English language skills by passing an approved test or being a national of a majority English-speaking country.
  • Maintenance Funds: You need to show you have enough funds to support yourself and any dependents during your stay in the UK. The required amount may vary based on your circumstances.


Application Process

Here’s a step-by-step guide to applying for a Tier 2 visa:

  1. Obtain a Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS): Secure a CoS from your UK employer. This document is crucial for your visa application.
  2. Complete the Online Application Form: Fill out the application form on the UK government’s official website, providing detailed personal and employment information.
  3. Pay the Application Fee: Submit the applicable visa application fee, which varies based on the visa duration and whether you’re applying from inside or outside the UK.
  4. Schedule a Biometric Appointment: After submitting your application, schedule an appointment at a visa application center to provide your fingerprints and photograph.
  5. Submit Supporting Documents: Gather and submit all necessary documents, including your passport, CoS, proof of English proficiency, and evidence of maintenance funds.
  6. Attend a Visa Interview (if required): You may be required to attend an interview to verify the information in your application.
  7. Wait for a Decision: After completing all steps, wait for a decision on your application. Processing times can vary, but you can track your application online.
  8. Collect Your Biometric Residence Permit (BRP): If approved, you’ll receive a BRP, which serves as proof of your right to stay in the UK.


Benefits of the Tier 2 Visa

  • Work in the UK: Gain valuable international work experience in a diverse environment.
  • Long-Term Employment: Stay in the UK for up to five years, with the option to extend your visa.
  • Path to Settlement: Apply for Indefinite Leave to Remain after meeting certain residency requirements.
  • Bring Your Family: Your spouse, partner, and children under 18 can join you as dependents, with the right to work and study.
  • Skill Development: Enhance your skills and learn new techniques in your field.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with professionals from around the world, building valuable relationships.


The UK Tier 2 visa is a valuable opportunity for skilled workers seeking employment in the UK. By understanding the requirements and following the correct application process, you can enhance your chances of success. Always consult official sources and seek professional advice to ensure you have the most accurate and up-to-date information.
